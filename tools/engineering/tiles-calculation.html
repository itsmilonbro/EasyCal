Helpful Tiling Tips

Measure Accurately

Always measure your room at multiple points as walls may not be perfectly straight.

Buy Extra Tiles

Purchase 10-15% extra tiles to account for cuts, breakage, and future repairs.

Check Tile Batch

Ensure all tiles are from the same batch to maintain consistent color and size.
